What's Gordy's Bakery
and Coffeehouse all about?

Gordy's Bakery and Coffeehouse is not your typical bakery. It is a food destination in Encinitas and is home of the famous plonk. What is a plonk, you ask? Gordy invented the plonk, a favorite of customers. If you think Gordy's Bakery sounds familiar, you might be remembering Gordy's wholesale bakery that was open about ten years ago. Now Gordy is back and better than ever in his bakery that opened July 2010.
Gordy's Bakery and Coffeehouse is quickly becoming a favorite spot of local athletes (Gordy is a runner and high school running coach by the way). Whether you're in the middle of a bike ride, just finished a run, or just want a snack make sure to stop by Gordy's!
